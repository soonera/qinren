Qin Ren

Hi, I am Qin Ren, a first-year Computer Science Ph.D. student at Stony Brook University, where I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Chenyu You. I received my B.S. in Biomedical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology and M.S. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from Tsinghua University. Previously, I spent great time at OpenMMLab and Tencent AI Lab.

My research lies in machine learning and its application to healthcare.
